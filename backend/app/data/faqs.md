# Guest FAQs

These are synthetic FAQs modelled after typical hotel-group support content. Used as RAG retrieval material.

## Check-in and check-out

Standard check-in is from 14:00 (some properties 15:00 — see individual property listings). Check-out is by 10:00 or 11:00 depending on the property. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability; please request at the front desk on arrival.

## Cancellation policy

Flexible-rate bookings can be cancelled up to 24 hours before check-in with no charge. Advance-purchase and non-refundable rates cannot be cancelled or modified. Cancellations within 24 hours of check-in are charged one night's stay.

## Children and extra beds

Children 12 and under stay free in existing bedding. Cots are available on request at no charge — please advise at booking. Rollaway beds are available at some properties (AUD 35/night) — subject to room type and availability.

## Pets

Most TFE-brand hotels do not accept pets, with the exception of certified service and assistance animals. Some Adina properties accept small dogs on a case-by-case basis — please contact the property directly.

## Parking

Parking is offered at most properties for an additional nightly fee. Rates vary by property and city — typical AUD 40-55/night. Some properties have on-site valet; others use a partner garage nearby. EV charging is available at Vibe Hotel Sydney and Vibe Hotel Melbourne.

## Wi-Fi

Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout all guest rooms and public areas, no login required after the initial captive portal at check-in.

## Loyalty programme

TFE Hotels Privilege members earn points on every direct booking (1 AUD = 1 point) and unlock perks like room upgrades, early check-in, and a welcome amenity from Silver tier. Joining is free at tfehotels.com/privilege.

## Late arrival

If you'll arrive after 22:00, please notify the property in advance. All TFE hotels have 24-hour reception, so late arrival is fine — but giving notice avoids any "no-show" risk on prepaid bookings.

## Group bookings

For bookings of 10 rooms or more, use the group bookings form at tfehotels.com/groups. A dedicated coordinator will respond within one business day.

## Accessibility

Accessible rooms with roll-in showers and grab rails are available at all TFE properties. Please request at the time of booking; quantities are limited. Service animals are always welcome.

## Breakfast

Breakfast options vary by property. Travelodge properties typically include continental breakfast in the room rate. Vibe and Adina properties offer breakfast as an add-on (typical AUD 28-35/person for full buffet).

## Payment methods

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX), debit cards, and Apple Pay / Google Pay at check-in. Direct bank transfer is supported for group and corporate bookings only.

## Lost property

Lost property is held for 30 days. Contact the property's front desk directly, or email lostproperty@tfehotels.com with your stay dates and a description. Return shipping is at the guest's cost.
